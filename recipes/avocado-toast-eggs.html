Recipe for avocado toast with scrambled eggs

Description

This is a reclipe for making a delicious toast topped with avocado and scarambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • Two slices of toast
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 10g of butter
  • one large avocado
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Cut avocado into thin slices and put aside
  2. Put butter in frying pan on a low heat
  3. Crack 4 eggs and put into frying pan
  4. Put toast in toaster
  5. Add salt and pepper to eggs, stir for a few minutes until eggs are done
  6. Butter toast
  7. pour eggs onto toast
  8. add avocado to toast
