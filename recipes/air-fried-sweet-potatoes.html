Recipe for air fried sweet potatoes

Description

This is a reclipe for delicious air fried sweet potatoes. A healthy and easy way to prepare sweet potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 200g of sweet potatoes
  • soy sauce
  • oilve oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. cut sweet potatoes into approx. one inch cubes
  2. Apply light drizzing of olive oil
  3. Add salt, pepper and other seasonings to taste
  4. Mix thoroughly
  5. Put into air fryer for 10 mins
  6. stir
  7. Back in the air fryer for another 10 mins
