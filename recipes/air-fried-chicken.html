Recipe for air fried chicken

Description

This is a reclipe for delicious air fried chicken. Air fried chicken is a much healthier alternative to deep fried chicken.

Ingredients

  • 500g of chicken legs, thighs or wings
  • 50g of flour
  • 10g of corn starch
  • vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Clean and dry your chicken
  2. Apply light drizzing of oil to chicken
  3. Dip chicken into bowl of corn starch
  4. Dip corn starch covered chicken into bowl of flour
  5. Put chicken into air fryer
  6. fry for 25 mins
